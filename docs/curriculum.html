Training Curriculum

Comprehensive 10-module training program for Newark H&HS staff

Real Educational Videos: Our training modules feature actual subject matter experts presenting authentic content specific to each topic area.

8-10 sessions, 1.5-2 hours each

Timeline

4-6 weeks completion

Blended learning with hands-on labs

Overview of Newark's health & homeless services, current challenges, goals of the AI system

Lecture + Case Study 2 hours

KPIs & Performance Management

Key performance indicators, submission workflows, dashboards, and metrics tracking

8

AI Agent & Tool Integration

Tool Demo + Labs 2 hours
9

Workflow & Escalation Protocols

End-to-end workflows: client arrival → form generation → case assignment → resolution

Scenario Drills 1.5 hours

Quality Assurance & Feedback Loop

Audits, error rates, user feedback, system improvement, updates
